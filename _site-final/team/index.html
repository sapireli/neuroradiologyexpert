Danny Benmoshe, MD

Specialty: Neurology

Expertise: Traumatic Brain and Spine Injury Clinical Management, EEG, EMG, NCV

Full bio

Dr. Danny Benmoshe is a diplomat of the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology who practices in Los Angeles, California. He is the principal investigator for the Brain Science Research Institute.

Dr. Benmoshe specializes in treating all neurological conditions, including cognitive conditions such as mild traumatic brain injuries, Alzheimer's disease, and other dementias. He also performs diagnostic tests, including EEG, EMG, and NCV. He is affiliated with Cedars-Sinai Medical Center.

He received his medical degree from Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University in 2005 and did his residency and fellowship at Harbor-UCLA Medical Center. He is fluent in Spanish, Farsi, Hebrew, and English. He has received excellent ratings from his patients for his thoroughness, clarity, and ability to answer questions. He is also recognized as a top neurologist in Los Angeles and a leader in specialized neurological care and brain health.

Contact: dbenmoshe@neuroexpertsgroup.com

Liorah Sabbah, PhD

Specialty: Neuropsychology

Expertise: Neuropsychological Evaluation of Traumatic Brain Injury, Dementia, Functional Neurological Disorders, and Complex Psychiatric Presentations

Full bio

Dr. Liorah Sabbah is a clinical neuropsychologist who practices in Los Angeles, California. Dr. Sabbah specializes in evaluating adults and older adults with neurological and medical challenges, including dementia, traumatic brain injury, functional neurological disorders, and complex psychiatric presentations.

She also provides psychotherapeutic interventions, helping individuals optimize cognitive performance and maintain quality of life as they age.

She holds an associate director of clinical training position at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center and an affiliation at California Rehabilitation Institute in inpatient rehabilitation. She received her doctorate degree from Suffolk University in 2018. She completed her internship at the Boston VA Healthcare System - Harvard Medical School program and her fellowship at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, followed by advanced fellowship training in TBI.

She is fluent in French and English. Dr. Sabbah's approach is grounded in empathy and compassion, creating a supportive space for growth, healing, and resilience.

Contact: lsabbah@neuroexpertsgroup.com

Daniel Fenton, PhD

Specialty: Neuropsychology

Expertise: Neuropsychological Evaluation of Traumatic Brain Injury, Cognitive Effects of Trauma and Emotional Injury, and Dementia

Full bio

Dr. Daniel Fenton is a clinical neuropsychologist based in Los Angeles, California. Dr. Fenton specializes in the assessment and treatment of individuals with brain injury, cognitive effects of trauma and emotional injury, and various forms of dementia.

He earned his doctorate in clinical psychology with a concentration in neuropsychology from the California School of Professional Psychology. He completed his internship in neuropsychology at the Iowa City VA Healthcare System, followed by advanced fellowship training at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center and additional specialized training in brain injury at California Rehabilitation Institute.

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Fenton has provided expert consultation in legal and forensic contexts, assisting numerous attorneys and law firms in achieving favorable case outcomes. His approach integrates scientific precision with compassion, helping clients regain clarity, resilience, and cognitive strength.

Contact: dfenton@neuroexpertsgroup.com

Nusha Bashardoust, Physician Assistant

Specialty: Neurology, Emergency Medicine, Dermatology

Expertise: Medical-Legal Evaluations (IME, QME, AME)

Full bio

Nusha Bashardoust has been a devoted healthcare provider since 2014, bringing extensive experience in Emergency Medicine and Dermatology, where she assists with medical-legal evaluations. She obtained her Bachelor's in Health Promotion and Disease Prevention and her Master of Physician Assistant Practice from the USC Keck School of Medicine, building a strong foundation in evidence-based, patient-centered care.

Her interest in neurology began in the emergency department, where she managed acute neurological cases and saw the significant impact neurological disorders have on patients. This passion led her to transition into neurology, where she now focuses on medical review and evaluation.

Alongside her clinical roles, Nusha has collaborated closely with a Dermatology physician on medical-legal assessments, including Independent Medical Examinations (IMEs), Qualified Medical Evaluations (QMEs), and Agreed Medical Evaluations (AMEs). Known for her analytical skills, attention to detail, and compassionate care, she continues to advance neurological assessment and patient care.

Contact: nbashardoust@neuroexpertsgroup.com
